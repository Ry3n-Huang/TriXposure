Quick Review Guide

ISO – Light Sensitivity

Lower ISO (100): Cleaner image, needs more light

Higher ISO (800+): Works in low light, adds noise

Always start with the lowest ISO that gives enough brightness.

Shutter Speed – Motion Control

Fast (1/1000s): Freezes action (sports/wildlife)

Slow (1/30s+): Creates motion blur (light trails)

Aperture – Depth & Light

Wide (f/1.8): Bright + blurry background (portraits)

Narrow (f/11): Darker + full focus (landscapes)
