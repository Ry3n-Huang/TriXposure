Aperture – Depth and Light in One Setting

Aperture controls the size of the opening in your lens, affecting:

  • A wider aperture (like f/1.8) lets in more light.
  • Smaller aperture (like f/16) reduces light.
  • Wider aperture = shallow depth of field (portraits).
  • Smaller aperture = greater depth of field (landscapes).
