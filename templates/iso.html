ISO – Controlling Light Sensitivity

ISO controls how sensitive your camera sensor is to light.

  • Lower ISO = cleaner image, ideal for well-lit scenes such as daytime.
  • Higher ISO = works in low light but adds noise (graininess).

***Tip: Always try lowest ISO possible for cleanest result.***

Click on a scene to see how ISO affects your photo and which setting works best.
