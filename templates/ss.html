Shutter Speed – Freezing or Blurring Motion

Fast Shutter (1/1000s to 1/4000s):

  • Freezes motion.
  • Great for taking sports photos.

Slow Shutter (1/30s to several seconds):

  • Creates motion blur or light trails.
  • Affects brightness: slower shutter = brighter photo.
  • Useful for night scenes, waterfalls, or creative motion blur.
